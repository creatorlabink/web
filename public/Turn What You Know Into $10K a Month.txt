CHAPTER 1: Discover Your Knowledge Asset

Before you create an e-book or a course, you must first identify the knowledge you already have that people are willing to learn from.

Many people believe they do not know enough to teach anything. The truth is that everyone has knowledge that can help someone else. The problem is that most people overlook their own experiences because those things feel normal to them.

What is normal to you may be life-changing for someone else.

Your knowledge asset is simply the information, experience, skill, lesson, or transformation you have that can help another person solve a problem or achieve a result.

Your knowledge asset can come from several places.

Skills
Things you know how to do.

Experiences
Things you have done or gone through.

Pain and struggles
Difficult situations you overcame.

Knowledge and expertise
Things you studied, practiced, or worked in.

For example, someone could create an e-book about:

How they started a small online business
How they overcame anxiety
How they lost weight
How they became confident speaking in public
How they learned a skill that others want to learn

The key idea is simple. People pay for solutions. If your knowledge can help someone avoid mistakes, save time, solve a problem, or achieve a result faster, then it has value.

In this step, you will use ChatGPT to help you identify the best topic for your first digital product.

Instead of guessing, you will use a prompt that helps ChatGPT evaluate your skills, experiences, and knowledge, and recommend the best product idea based on demand and your level of understanding.

Demand is very important. A topic that many people want to learn will usually sell better than a topic that only a few people are interested in.

However, you must also have enough knowledge about the topic to guide others.

The prompt below will help you analyse both factors.

CHAPTER 2: Turn Your Knowledge Into an E-Book Using ChatGPT

Now that you have chosen your product idea, the next step is to turn that idea into a clear, structured, and valuable e-book.

Many people get stuck at this stage because they believe writing a book is difficult. The truth is that creating an e-book today is much easier because you can use tools like ChatGPT to help you organize your knowledge and turn it into well-structured content.

Your role is not to let ChatGPT write random content for you. Your role is to guide it with the right prompts so it helps you transform your knowledge into a useful guide for your readers.

Your e-book does not need to be 200 pages. In fact, many successful digital products are between 20 and 40 pages as long as they solve a real problem clearly and practically.

The goal of this step is simple. You will use ChatGPT to create the structure of your book and then generate the content chapter by chapter.

Step 1: Create Your E-Book Outline

Before writing the book, you must first create a clear outline. An outline is simply the structure of your book. It shows the chapters and the flow of information from the beginning to the end.

Copy the prompt below and paste it into ChatGPT.

Prompt to Generate Your Book Outline

"I want to create an e-book based on the following topic:

[Insert the product idea ChatGPT recommended in CHAPTER 1]

Please create a clear and practical e-book outline that will help a beginner understand and solve this problem.

The outline should include:

• A compelling introduction
• 6 to 10 clear chapters
• Actionable steps inside each chapter
• A conclusion that motivates the reader to take action

The book should be simple, practical, and focused on helping readers achieve a real result.

Do not make the chapters too academic. Focus on clear explanations, practical advice, and step-by-step guidance."

Once ChatGPT generates the outline, review it carefully. If you want to adjust anything, you can ask ChatGPT to refine or simplify it.

When you are happy with the outline, move to the next step.

Step 2: Generate Each Chapter

Instead of asking ChatGPT to write the entire book at once, generate it one chapter at a time. This allows you to review the content and make sure it sounds natural and helpful.

Use the prompt below for each chapter.

Prompt to Write Each Chapter

"I am writing an e-book titled:

[Insert your e-book title]

Please write Chapter [Insert chapter number] based on the following outline:

[Paste the chapter title from your outline]

Requirements:

• Write in a simple and conversational tone
• Make the chapter practical and easy to understand
• Include examples where necessary
• Provide actionable advice readers can apply immediately
• Avoid sounding robotic or overly academic

The chapter should feel like a helpful guide written by someone who has real experience with the topic.

But before writing here are my own person ideas, thoughts and experiences you can weave into writing this chapter, use them but add your own aswell and structure it right"

Repeat this process for every chapter in your book.

Step 3: Add More of Your Personal Insight

This is very important.

Your readers want to learn from you, not just from artificial intelligence. After generating each chapter, read through it and add your own insights.

You can do this by:

• Adding personal stories
• Sharing mistakes you made
• Explaining lessons you learned
• Giving examples from your real life

This makes your e-book authentic and valuable.

Remember, people connect with real experiences.

Step 4: Create a Strong Introduction and Conclusion

Your introduction should help readers understand three things.

What problem this book will help them solve
Why the problem matters
What result they will achieve after reading the book

Your conclusion should encourage readers to take action immediately instead of just reading the information.

Prompt to Create a Powerful Introduction

"Write a compelling introduction for an e-book titled:

[Insert your e-book title]

The introduction should:

• Explain the problem readers are facing
• Show them that a solution is possible
• Explain what they will learn from the book
• Motivate them to continue reading."

Prompt to Create a Strong Conclusion

"Write a motivating conclusion for my e-book titled:

[Insert your e-book title]

The conclusion should inspire readers to take action on what they have learned and remind them that progress comes from applying knowledge."

Your Goal for This Step

By the end of this step, you should have a complete e-book draft with:

• A clear introduction
• Several helpful chapters
• Practical guidance for readers
• A motivating conclusion

Do not worry about perfection. Your goal is progress.

In the next step, you will learn how to design a professional-looking book cover and create a realistic 3D mockup that makes your e-book look attractive and credible when you start selling it.

Action Task

Use the prompts in this step to generate your book outline and write each chapter.

When you are finished, combine all chapters into a single document. This will become the first version of your e-book.

Stan Store allows you to sell digital products directly from a single link that you can place in your social media bio. When people click the link, they will see your product and can purchase it immediately.

Your goal in this step is to create your store, upload your e-book, and generate the link you will later place in your TikTok bio.

CHAPTER 6: How to get 1,000 TikTok Followers in 14 Days or less.

Before your Stan Store link can become clickable on TikTok, your account must have at least 1,000 followers.

This is an important milestone because once you reach 1,000 followers, you can add a clickable link in your bio that takes people directly to your store where they can buy your e-book.

Your goal in this step is simple.

Reach 1,000 followers as quickly as possible while preparing your page to start attracting real viewers who will eventually buy your product.

One of the fastest ways to reach this milestone is by using TikTok grow hosts.

Step 1: Understand How Grow Hosts Work

Grow hosts are TikTok creators who go live specifically to help people grow their followers.

During their live sessions, they encourage viewers to follow each other so everyone’s follower count increases.

People in the live stream support each other by:

Following each other
Commenting actively
Sharing the live stream
Liking the live stream
Sending gifts to the host

The host usually rewards the most active supporters by calling out their usernames or pinning their accounts so other viewers can easily follow them.

The more visible you are in the live stream, the more followers you can gain.

Step 2: Choose the Right Grow Hosts

Not all grow hosts are equally effective.

You should target hosts that have between 50 and 200 people in their live sessions.

Here is why this range is important.

If the live has fewer than 50 people, it will be difficult to gain many followers because the audience is too small.

If the live has more than 200 people, it becomes harder for the host to notice you unless you are spending a lot of money on gifts.

The 50 to 200 viewer range is usually the best balance. It is active enough to grow quickly, but still small enough for you to be noticed.

Step 3: Be Highly Active in the Live Stream

To grow followers quickly, you must be visible in the live stream.

You can do this by:

Commenting frequently
Praising the host
Following people who follow you
Sharing the live stream
Liking the live stream

The more active you are, the more likely the host will mention your name or highlight your account.

When this happens, many people in the live stream will follow you.

Step 4: Use TikTok Gifts Strategically

Many grow hosts prioritize people who support the live stream through gifts.

You may need to purchase TikTok coins so you can send gifts during the live session.

This increases your chances of being noticed and helps you appear in the host’s top supporter list.

When you appear in the top supporters list, your account becomes more visible to everyone watching the live.

This often results in more followers.

Step 5: Set a Daily Growth Target

Your target should be around 150 followers per day.

If you spend about 2 hours in the right grow host live streams and remain active, reaching this goal is very possible.

At this pace, you can reach 1,000 followers in about one to two weeks.

Step 6: Use Additional Ways to Gain Followers

Grow hosts should not be your only strategy. You should combine it with other simple methods.

Ask family and friends to follow your TikTok page.
Share your TikTok profile with your contacts.
Post your TikTok page on your other social media platforms.

These small actions can give your account an early boost.

Step 7: Promote Your Videos

You can also use TikTok’s promotion feature to boost some of your videos.

Promoting your content helps more people discover your page and increases your chances of gaining followers quickly.

Step 8: Create Viral Content

Another powerful way to gain followers is by posting videos that perform well on TikTok.

When a video goes viral, thousands of people can discover your page.

Some of those viewers will follow you because they want to see more of your content.

Focus on creating short, engaging videos that:

Teach something useful
Share an interesting idea
Tell a relatable story
Solve a simple problem

The more engaging your content is, the more likely TikTok will show it to a larger audience.

Your Goal for This Step

By the end of this step, you should have:

Reached at least 1,000 followers on TikTok
Built early visibility on your page
Prepared your account to activate your clickable link in bio

Once you reach 1,000 followers, you will add your Stan Store link to your bio so viewers can easily access your product.

In the next step, you will learn how to turn your followers into buyers using viral content, live sessions, and emotional storytelling.

Action Task

Spend about 2 hours daily participating in grow host live streams.

Stay highly active, support the host, and interact with other viewers.

Combine this with posting videos and sharing your TikTok page with friends and family until you reach your first 1,000 followers.


CHAPTER 7: Convert Followers Into Buyers Using Content and Live Streams

Congratulations! By now, your TikTok account has at least 1,000 followers, and your Stan Store link is now clickable in your bio. The final step is to turn your audience into paying customers.

This step focuses on creating content and live sessions that build trust, connect emotionally, and guide your followers to purchase your e-book.

Step 1: Use Emotional Storytelling

People buy when they feel a connection or see themselves in your story.

* Share personal experiences related to your e-book topic.
* Talk about struggles you overcame and lessons you learned.
* Highlight the transformation your e-book can provide.

For example, if your e-book teaches beginners how to create digital products, share a story of your first failed attempt, how you learned from it, and the success you eventually achieved.

Step 2: Go Live Strategically

Live streams are powerful for selling because they allow direct interaction.

* Schedule regular live sessions at consistent times.
* Offer value during your live: tips, mini-lessons, or Q&A sessions.
* Softly promote your e-book by mentioning how it can help viewers achieve results.
* Encourage viewers to click the link in your bio.

Step 3: Make Viral Videos That Lead to Sales

Viral content increases your visibility and brings new potential buyers to your profile.

* Keep videos short, engaging, and practical.
* Include a clear takeaway or actionable tip.
* End with a call to action like, “Check the link in my bio to learn more.”

Step 4: Use Stories to Connect Emotionally

TikTok stories and posts let you connect with followers on a personal level.

* Share mini successes or breakthroughs from your e-book content.
* Post behind-the-scenes moments of creating content or the product.
* Ask questions that get followers to engage and relate to your topic.

Step 5: Maintain Consistency and Provide Value

Even after you start selling, your audience expects consistent content.

* Post regularly to keep followers engaged.
* Continue giving value that aligns with your product.
* Answer comments and messages to build trust.

Step 6: Track and Optimize

Monitor your sales and engagement to see what works best.

* Identify videos or live sessions that drive the most clicks to your link.
* Repeat strategies that perform well and refine those that don’t.
* Adjust your content to meet the needs and interests of your followers.

Your Goal for This Step

By the end of this step, you should be able to:
